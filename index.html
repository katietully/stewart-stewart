Audubon views and more by

Jane E. Goldman

This month Stewart & Stewart is proud to feature the nineteen exceptional screenprint editions Jane E. Goldman created at Stewart & Stewart in the past three decades. The representative image to the left, Audubon June, is the second screenprint edition in the artist’s Audubon series, including eight related editions completed between 2003 and 2010. Inspired by John James Audubon's seminal text Birds of America, Audubon June is a luminous 16-color screenprint, hand printed in a limited edition of 64 impressions on Rives BFK 100% cotton paper, each measuring 21.75”h. x 29.75”w. At this date, impressions from each of the eight Audubon editions remain available as well as other select screenprints completed since 1983. To review our complete inventory of fine prints by Jane E. Goldman, click on the image to the left.
